frequently asked questions

how to cancel?

You can cancel your order anytime before it is shipped. Simply log into your account, go to “My Orders,” select the order you want to cancel, and click “Cancel.”

is there any vacancy?

Yes! We are always looking for passionate and talented people. Check our Careers page regularly for job openings and internship opportunities.

what are payment methods?

We accept payments via credit/debit cards, mobile banking, PayPal, and cash on delivery in select areas for your convenience.

how to claim coupon codes?

Apply your coupon code during checkout in the “Discount Code” field. The discount will be automatically applied to your total before payment.

what are the age requirements?

You must be at least 13 years old to create an account and 18 or older to make purchases independently. Minors can order with parental guidance.
